About Me

Well, first off my name is Dominique, and I currently live in San Fransisco, CA. My life has been up and down the past few years, from having a fairly lucrative job, to moving to China and teaching young learners how to speak English. I am a 26 year old who has spent most of his life studying and working away. I have always had a strong interest in computers, I even how to build my own desktop when i was sixteen years old.

Until recently i just thought of it as a hobby and a way to pass time, however since moving to San Fransisco, I've realised that i can make this "hobby" become something i look forward to doing every day at work! I became motivated and joined Udacity to do just that! I have high expectations for my sucess, and strive to use my experiences inside and outside of the technology world to push myself and those around me.
